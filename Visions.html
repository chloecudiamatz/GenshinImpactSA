This page will explain the Visions a character can get in each Region introduced in Genshin Impact

- The Known Visions to obtain in Teyvat -

Anemo

  • Anemo characters obtain their Visions after showing a deep desire for freedom, achieving peace after hardship, or upholding their ideals despite opposition.

Cryo

  • Cryo users gain their Visions by rebelling against fate or strong traditions, often after conflict, hardship, or refusing to conform to expectations.

Dendro

  • Dendro Visions are granted to those with strong connections to knowledge, growth, and helping others, tied to wisdom and the interconnectedness of life.

Electro

  • Electro Visions are given to individuals with intense desires for power or ambition, often challenging norms or seeking to reshape their reality.

Geo

  • Geo Visions are granted to those with unwavering ambition, loyalty, and dedication, often tied to contracts, duty, and the pursuit of long-term goals.

Hydro

  • Hydro Vision users are motivated by justice, truth, or duty. Some gain them through selfless acts, others during crises while defending themselves or others.

Pyro

  • Pyro Vision bearers receive their element due to intense passion or ambition, reflecting their fiery personality and life circumstances.
