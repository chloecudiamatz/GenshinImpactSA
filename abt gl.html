About Genshin Impact

A Magical Journey Through Teyvat

Who Created Genshin Impact?

  • Genshin Impact was developed and published by miHoYo, a Chinese video game company that later rebranded internationally as HoYoverse. They are also known for creating Honkai Impact 3rd and Honkai: Star Rail.

When Was It Released?

  • The game officially launched on September 28, 2020 for PC, PlayStation 4, iOS, and Android. It later expanded to PlayStation 5, and a Nintendo Switch version is still in development.

How Did It Become Popular?

  • At release, Genshin Impact surprised players with its free-to-play open-world RPG style, stunning graphics, and deep lore. It gained worldwide recognition through social media, word of mouth, and comparisons to Zelda: Breath of the Wild.
  • Over the years, it has kept its popularity with frequent content updates, new regions, limited-time events, and new characters that keep the world of Teyvat exciting.

What Is the Game About?

  • Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG where players take on the role of the Traveler, a mysterious adventurer searching for their lost twin. Along the way, you meet companions, explore the seven nations of Teyvat, and uncover the secrets of the gods known as the Archons.
  • The game combines exploration, puzzle-solving, elemental combat, and character collection through its gacha system.
